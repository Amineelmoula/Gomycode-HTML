LEARN ABOUT OUR OUR CHANGES AFTER COVID-19

In the light of COVID-19, we are protecting the safety of our clients and employees by spacing out our styling stations more than six feet apart. We installed a new air conditioning system with a UV light sanitizer to ensure the purest ambient air quality. We took additional measures for blow-drying by keeping our windows and doors open for better air circulation. Frequent disinfection will be performed on often-touched areas. We will be disinfecting our equipment, each station, and its surrounding areas between uses. Please be advised that blow-dry appointments are no longer available for booking. We only accept already booked blow-dry appointments.
